THE AWARDS
2022 IIHS Top Safety Pick+

Four years in a row, we have been recognized for our commitment to customer safety. Five models — G70 (built after June 2021), G80, G90, GV70, and GV80 — have all received the 2022 IIHS TOP SAFETY PICK+ rating, meeting the organization’s high standards for road safety. We’re honored to earn this yet again on our US models, and we have our sights set on achieving even more in 2023.

MOTORTREND NAMES GENESIS GV70 2022 MotorTrend SUV OF THE YEAR®

(North America)

The Genesis GV70 was named as 2022 MotorTrend SUV of the Year® (North America) with excellent performance in all categories of design, engineering, efficiency, and safety.

GV60 WINS 2022 RED DOT AWARD FOR PRODUCT DESIGN

The Genesis GV60, the brand’s first model powered by dedicated EV platform, won a prestigious honor at this year’s Red Dot Award: Product Design 2022 in the cars and motorcycles category, hosted by Design Zentrum Nordrhein Westfalen in Germany.

GENESIS X CONCEPT WINS ‘BEST OF THE BEST’ RED DOT DESIGN AWARD

The electric concept car, the Genesis X Concept, won a Red Dot: Best of the Best award in the Mobility and Transportation segment of the Design Concept category at the 2021 Red Dot Design Awards, hosted by Design Zentrum Nordrhein Westfalen in Germany.

2020 GOOD DESIGN® AWARD

Striking the perfect balance between athleticism and elegance, both the G80 and GV80 took home prizes at the 2020 GOOD DESIGN® Awards.
